PAYMENT NOTICE

Dear Parents,

We kindly request you to pay the fees either online or by visiting the school. The school administration is open from 10:00 am to 3:00 pm on weekdays. To pay the amount online, you can use either e-Sewa, connect IPS, or direct Bank transfer. The details are given below. Do not forget to mail your payment voucher or screenshot.


Through eSewa

  • Search for School Fee on e-Sewa using your mobile or PC.
  • Select the option School Fee Payment.
  • Type and select NeuroTech Academy from the list.
  • Fill out all required details in the form.
  • Ensure the Student Name and Grade are correctly filled in.
  • Review the details before proceeding.
  • Click Confirm to complete the payment.

Through Mobile Banking, Online Banking or Connect IPS:

  • Payments can be made directly to our bank account.
  • You can use mobile banking, online banking, or Connect IPS.
  • Log in to your preferred payment platform.
  • Enter our bank account details accurately.
  • Fill out all the fields in the payment form.
  • Student Name and Grade are compulsory.
  • Write both in the remarks section before submitting.
